Transformation Example
Email Restructuring — Before & After
Prompt: Transform unstructured field email into a professional, action-oriented message
Before — Original Email Unstructured
From: ravi.kulkarni@ops.in
To: ops-team@mumbaibus.in
Subject: Bus issue yesterday - need help
Hi, so yesterday our bus was having some problems you see and the driver told me there was engine noise and also the passengers were complaining and then we had to stop midway on route 12 near Bandra station so the passengers had to get off and also there was some delay because we couldn't reach the workshop and we tried calling but nobody picked up and also the route was affected for like 2 hours I think maybe more and I wanted to flag this to you guys and also we need to do something about the workshop response time they are really slow lately hope you can help thanks
1 No subject clarity — "Bus issue" gives no actionable info; reader cannot prioritise without opening the email.
2 Key data buried — bus ID, route number, exact time, passenger count are all absent or implied.
3 No clear ask — reader cannot determine what action is required or who should take it.
4 Mixed tone — informal, stream-of-consciousness writing is inappropriate for an incident report in a professional ops context.
After — Structured Email Action-oriented
From: ravi.kulkarni@ops.in
To: ops-team@mumbaibus.in
Subject: [ACTION REQUIRED] Engine Failure — Bus MH-04-CD-3344, Route 12, 13 Mar 2025
Situation
Bus MH-04-CD-3344 experienced engine failure at 14:35 on 13 March 2025, near Bandra Station on Route 12. The vehicle was stopped safely and all passengers were evacuated. No injuries reported.
Impact
Route 12 was non-operational for 2 hours 15 minutes (14:35–16:50). Estimated passenger impact: ~120 commuters. Workshop was unreachable during initial 45-minute window.
Action Required
Workshop team to inspect vehicle and confirm repair timeline by EOD today (13 Mar).
Ops Head to confirm if alternate vehicle can be deployed for evening Route 12 service.
Timeline
Vehicle must be operational by 06:00 AM on 15 March 2025 to avoid further route disruption.
Ravi Kulkarni  |  Hub Manager, Bandra Depot  |  +91 98200 33412
1 Clear subject line — bus ID + route + date makes the email immediately actionable without opening it.
2 Situation / Impact / Action / Timeline — scannable structure allows ops manager to triage in seconds.
3 Specific data — times, passenger count, vehicle ID, and duration of impact are all explicitly stated.
4 Named CTAs with deadlines — each action item has a clear owner and a specific deadline.
