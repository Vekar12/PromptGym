1. Scope & Applicability

This SOP applies to all hub managers, drivers, and fleet supervisors across the Mumbai Division. It covers all incidents involving fleet vehicles during operational hours, including accidents, breakdowns, and passenger-related incidents. All personnel must be familiar with this SOP before commencing field operations.

2. Incident Reporting Steps

Step Action Responsible Party Timeline Tool / Form
1
Incident occurs — report immediately
Contact Hub Manager via WhatsApp or phone call
 Driver Within 5 min WhatsApp to Hub Manager
2
Assess severity — classify incident
Classify as Minor / Major / Critical per Appendix A
 Hub Manager Within 10 min Incident Classification Chart (Appendix A)
3
Secure scene — ensure passenger safety
Call police (100) if Critical; cordon off area; do not move vehicle
 Driver + Hub Manager Immediate —
4
Document the incident
Fill Incident Report Form OPS-IR-01 with full details
 Hub Manager Within 30 min Form OPS-IR-01 (iLens)
5
Notify stakeholders
Inform Ops Head — mandatory for Major and Critical incidents
 Hub Manager Within 1 hour Phone + WhatsApp
6
Submit to system — upload form
Upload completed form OPS-IR-01 to iLens portal
 Hub Manager Within 2 hours iLens Portal
7
File insurance claim — if vehicle damage
Initiate claim via ICICI Lombard portal; attach Form OPS-IR-01
 Insurance Coordinator Within 24 hours ICICI Lombard Portal

3. Escalation Matrix

Tier Trigger Condition Escalate To Response Time
Minor No injury; vehicle damage <₹5,000 Hub Manager handles independently Same day
Major Passenger/driver injury; damage >₹5,000 Hub Manager → Ops Head Within 1 hour
Critical Fatality; damage >₹50,000; media attention Ops Head → MD + Legal Within 30 minutes

Appendix A — Incident Classification Table

Minor
  • Tyre puncture (no delay >30 min)
  • Minor fender contact
  • Passenger complaint — non-injury
  • Dashboard warning light
Major
  • Passenger injury (non-critical)
  • Significant vehicle damage (>₹5,000)
  • Collision with another vehicle
  • Route delay >60 min due to breakdown
Critical
  • Fatality — passenger or third party
  • Major fire or explosion
  • Damage >₹50,000
  • Media or MSRTC escalation
