SLA Pause Conditions

The SLA clock is paused (and restarted upon resolution) in the following scenarios. Pause must be logged in iLens with a reason code at the time of occurrence.

  • 1 Customer document submission pending — pause logged in iLens with reason code DOC-PEND
  • 2 VAHAN API or external government registry unavailability exceeding 4 continuous hours
  • 3 Legal holiday declared in the jurisdiction applicable to the property being assessed
  • 4 Customer-requested hold — written request (email or physical) required before pause is logged
Breach Reporting: Breach reporting is automated via iLens dashboards and refreshed every 4 hours during business hours. All breaches exceeding 2 days beyond the defined SLA threshold require a written explanation note to be submitted within 24 hours by the stage owner. Repeated breaches (>3 in a month) trigger a formal corrective action review.
