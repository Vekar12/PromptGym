Executive Summary

The Indian fleet management SaaS market is dominated by 4–5 established players with Fleetx holding the strongest market position at ~34% share, primarily due to deep NBFC integrations and enterprise-grade analytics. A significant whitespace exists in the SME segment (<50 vehicle fleets), where no current player offers sub-₹2,000/vehicle/month pricing, despite confirmed operator willingness to pay at ₹1,200–1,800. Driver behavior analytics is emerging as the fastest-differentiating feature category, with a 67% YoY increase in user mentions across review platforms.
